Canonical list of positioning angles for [[shameless-snacks|Shameless Snacks]] creative. Every ad, script, LP, or email should lead with one primary angle and may layer a secondary. The primary angle across the brand is always **fiber-first** — sub-angles route through fiber as the mechanism (see [[fiber-first-positioning]]).

## The 15 Angles

Each angle now includes a **Q4 2025 performance grade** from the Creative Strategy Review (source: $10.4M total spend, 10,000+ creatives). Account average CPA: $116.62.

### Tier 4 — Deprioritize (The Influencer Trap)

**Generic Influencer / Testimonial ("Other"):** Currently consuming **~68% of total spend ($7M+)** at $157 CPA and 0.76 ROAS. This is the account's biggest inefficiency. Generic "I love this snack" influencer content without a sharp angle hook is bleeding budget.

**Action:** Cut generic influencer budget by 50% immediately. All future influencer content must use a Tier 1 angle hook ("26g Fiber," "Travel Hack," "Swap your candy") rather than generic "Yum" reactions. Move remaining influencer spend to MOF/BOF only — influencer content performs at $96 CPA in retargeting vs. $162 in prospecting.

## Angle Layering ("Power Stacks")

Q4 2025 data confirms that stacking angles significantly improves performance compared to single-angle ads.

### Proven Power Stacks

| Stack | CPA | ROAS | Funnel Stage | Why It Works |
|-------|-----|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Fiber + Weight** | $59 | — | Prospecting (best cold traffic hook) | Promises a result AND explains how |
| **Fiber + Digestive** | $69 | 1.41 | Retargeting (highest ROAS stack) | Gut health benefit lands after brand awareness |
| **Convenience (standalone)** | $62 | 0.84 | Prospecting | Solves an immediate daily pain point |
| **Fiber + Weight** | $64 | — | Retargeting | Consistent across both funnel stages |

### Working Combos

- **Fiber-First + Macro Shock** — lead with the stat, land with disbelief ("the whole bag is 70 calories?!")
- **Fiber-First + Weight Management** — fiber as the mechanism, weight goal as the payoff
- **Guilt-Free + Taste** — permission + proof the product delivers
- **GLP-1 + Appetite Control** — audience hook + functional benefit
- **Diet Rebellion + Fiber-First** — emotional hook + rational proof
- **Guilt-Free + Fiber-First** — "Eat the whole bag AND get 26g of fiber" (combine emotional + functional)

### Toxic Combination

**Guilt-Free + Weight ($113 CPA)** — feels like a generic "diet candy" pitch. Lacks the unique fiber differentiator. Avoid this pairing.

## Compliance Notes

- **GLP-1 angle:** Never name Ozempic/Wegovy/Mounjaro. Say "natural satiety" or "supports fullness naturally." See [[compliance-guardrails]].
- **Appetite Control:** "Promotes fullness" and "supports satiety" are safe. "Appetite suppressant" is BANNED (2026-06-30 — the 2026-04-15 permission was revoked). Keep craving relief first-person/situational.
- **Weight Management:** Weight-loss narrative framing is permitted per 2026-04-15 policy. Hard stops: "melt fat," "burn fat," transformation imagery.
- **Digestive Health:** "Supports regularity" is safe. "Cures constipation" is not. See [[compliance-guardrails]].

## Novel Hooks (from ChatGPT export)

- **"14 bags comparison"** — "This tiny bag of candy from the store has MORE SUGAR than 14 bags of Shameless Snacks combined. FOURTEEN." Comparison format not yet tested.
- **"Amazon #1"** — "Amazon's #1 Keto Gummies Are Here!" Third-party credibility hook.
- **"QVC Featured"** — "Featured 3 Times on QVC — Here's Why!" Underutilized social proof.
- **"Caught Cheating" skit** — Boyfriend comes home, girlfriend is "caught" with Shameless. Cheating = eating candy. Full dialogue skit concept.
- **Doctor testimonial** — "As a clinician, I often see patients fall short on daily fiber..." Clinical voice for trust-building. Near compliance boundary — keep factual.
- **"Muse formula"** — Find a competitor's winning ad structure, adapt it for Shameless. Documented creative process for systematic angle generation.

## 2026-04-24 New Angle Additions

Source: competitor-research-deep-2026-04-23.md (v2), competitor-research-v3-addendum-2026-04-24.md (v3 + v3.1). Three new angles surfaced from deep competitor research. Each is a flanking position against the contested GLP-1 fiber lane ([[glp1-dosing-companion]]) — Shameless should NOT compete head-on in "we're safe with your GLP-1" claims now that Metamucil, BelliWelli, Supergut, TUSHY, Peachy, and ColonBroom are all in-lane.

### Craving Control via Fiber (GLP-1 proxy)

Prebiotic fiber → SCFA → satiety → craving management. Framed as the natural-food mechanism behind what GLP-1 peers describe as "quieted food noise" without naming drugs. ("Food noise" itself is allowed as lifestyle/on-screen copy; just keep it grounded in the fiber mechanism, not drug framing.) Lands for Maggie + Linda (menopause) + Dave (blood sugar).

- **Entry phrase**: "Fiber keeps me full" / "I stopped thinking about the kitchen at 9pm" (peer-vocab; see [[voice-of-customer]])
- **Why now**: Supergut is explicitly claiming the SCFA→GLP-1 pathway at $25–32/bottle. Shameless's 26 g fiber per bag and candy format is a stronger flank than a direct head-to-head "we also do SCFA" claim.
- **Compliance**: Route through fiber mechanism, never name GLP-1 drugs, use HCP-consult hedge when approaching Rx-class language. See [[glp1-dosing-companion]].
- **Script anchor**: April 2026 batch Script 2 ("Late Night Cravings") and Script 9 ("GLP-1 Constipation Fix") — see [[script-leaderboard#April 2026 Batch]].

### Not Metamucil (direct comparison)

Metamucil's structure/function claim requires 7 g soluble fiber/day across 3 servings. Real-sugar SKU at 3 servings ≈ 33–45 g sugar/day. Their gummies deliver only 1.5–3 g each. Shameless: **26 g fiber / 3 g sugar / 70 cal in one bag**. The math is the angle.

- **Hook example (from v2 hook bank)**: *"Metamucil needs three servings and 45 grams of sugar to try to match one bag of Shameless. Do the math."*
- **Format**: 1:1 and 4:5 static + LP hero block. Side-by-side visual.
- **Compliance**: Substantiate sugar figure from current Metamucil packaging before running any label-comparison ad.
- See [[hook-framework#2026-04-24 Competitor-Sourced Hook Bank]] v2.1, v3.2.

### Timeline-of-Effect (TUSHY DR Pattern)

**Borrowed DR structure** from TUSHY Reboot's product page. Source: competitor-research-v3-addendum-2026-04-24.md. Shameless should absorb this pattern because it replaces abstract "take daily" with a concrete, checkable promise.

TUSHY's canonical pattern:

- 1–6 hours: Gut = activated.
- 12–48 hours: Movement.
- 3–7 days: Gut chatty, fiber doing its thing.
- 2+ weeks: Regularity, unlocked.

**Shameless adaptation** (hook v3.8): *"1–6 hours: your gut wakes up. 12–48 hours: movement. 2+ weeks: no more thinking about fiber. 26 grams in a bag. That's it."*

- **Effort**: LOW (copy rework on LP + FAQ)
- **Risk**: LOW-MED — compliance review on timeline-of-effect language. Keep "may" / "typical" hedges. No disease claims.
- **Use case**: LP hero, FAQ, creator scripts, subscribe-flow nudge.
- See [[brand-voice#Borrowed DR Patterns (2026-04-24)]] and [[shameless-landing-page-strategy]].

### Not a Supplement — Candy-as-Fiber

This is a **repositioning, not a new angle** — it hardens the existing Guilt-Free + Fiber-First frame into an explicit anti-supplement stance. Gap 7 in v2.

Peer narrative (r/Ozempic, r/Supplements, IBS Freedom Group attack ads) is increasingly anti-supplement: *"I just eat whole grains and lots of fruit and vegetables and I never have to take a supplement."* RestartYourGut actively sells an anti-fiber-supplement narrative.

Most customers who reject Metamucil / Benefiber / Grüns / ColonBroom are rejecting the *supplement identity*, not the molecule. Shameless's candy formulation lets it step around the whole supplement-rejection wave.

- **Hook examples**: *"We're not a supplement. We're candy that happens to have 26 g of fiber."* / *"Tired of your 'fiber supplement'? So were we. So we made fiber candy instead."*
- **Compliance**: Confirm DSHEA food vs. dietary-supplement classification with legal before leaning all-in. Formulation is already candy-adjacent; this is alignment, not invention.
