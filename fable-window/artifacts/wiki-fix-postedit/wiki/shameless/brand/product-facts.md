### Sourness Calibration

The brand's internal sour scale (1-5) does not match customer experience. Customers calibrate against Sour Patch Kids (6/10) and Warheads (10/10). Shameless sits at roughly 3-4/10 on that scale. The "Super Sour" branding overpromises. See [[flavor-strategy#The Sourness Problem]].

### Known Formulation Issues

- **Watermelon soap/chemical taste:** Confirmed across all 5 channels, multi-year. Not a batch anomaly. Extends to some Candy Carnival flavors (blueberry named).
- **Erythritol concern:** Growing customer backlash citing Cleveland Clinic cardiovascular study. See [[objection-library#11. Erythritol Safety (Cleveland Clinic Study)]].
- **Sweetness aftertaste:** Sucralose drives a "sickeningly fake sweet" complaint cluster in 2-3 star reviews.
