# 2026 creative strategy, operational cut

Distilled from the 2026-04 creative-strategy research package (8 deep dives: Meta, TikTok, snacks/GLP-1, ops/testing, Andromeda, fiber positioning, founder ads, food psychology; ~80 sources, compiled 2026-04-30). Every tactic below was cross-checked against the Shameless winner archive via [[feedback_winner_patterns_2026H1]] (v2, 334 winners + 635 variant subtasks + BQ60) and [[feedback-visual-winner-canon]] (29 read static creatives).

**Why:** the research corpus is 230KB of theory and operator lore. Most of it is directionally right; some of it is contradicted by what actually wins in the SHA account. This file keeps only what a brief can use, and marks which claims have SHA-money behind them.

**How to apply:** brief against CONFIRMED-BY-DATA tactics by default. Treat THEORY-ONLY as exploration bets to be labeled as such (per winner-patterns rule). When research and the winner archive disagree, the winner archive wins for SHA.

**Read first, one hard rule:** statics still out-earn video in the SHA account (0.86 vs 0.68 ROAS, BQ60) even though the research corpus pushes a 40% video format mix. The research mix is a category-wide default; SHA's proven lane is the static + headline stack. Do not let the research talk you off statics.

## CONFIRMED-BY-DATA (research tactic + SHA winner evidence agree)

1. **Iterate a live winner, change exactly one variable.** Default brief is one lever swapped (hook, headline, image, intro) on a proven body, not a fresh concept. Research: 70/30 iteration/net-new, "20 hooks on 1 body." SHA: 43% of winners carry a Retro token; HookTest variants ship at 71%, the single highest keep rate of any test type. Apply: hooks-on-a-proven-body is the cheapest slot in the account; make it the default, size new concepts as the minority lane.

2. **Static + headline stack is the winner factory.** 10 headlines on a proven Problem-Headline-Benefit layout, shipped weekly, before commissioning video. Research: stat-shock pack grids, 10 variants in 2 days. SHA: 145/334 winners are static; statics 0.86 ROAS vs video 0.68 (BQ60); "every winner but one was a static" (weekly 2026-06-22). Apply: brief static batches at 3 to 4 variants per survivor you need; visual template = urgency banner sandwich, comparison table, or macro close-up (see visual canon).

3. **Lead with fiber quantified against a known food.** "More fiber than 3 cups of broccoli," "26g vs 0g in a Snickers." Research: cleanest compliance-safe stat-shock in the category. SHA: 75 fiber winners; "MORE FIBER THAN 3 CUPS OF BROCCOLI" recurs 8x; FiberThere'sNoExcuse is #2 all-time at $26k; fiber statics keep at 42% (double the offer lane); comparison-table static is the #1 visual spender ($15.3k). Apply: 26g canonical; verify the gram math counts only FDA-counted fibers before the claim runs (prebiotic and acacia/gum arabic mostly do NOT count on the panel, class-action bait).

4. **Address GLP-1 by symptom and consequence, never the drug.** "For the days you can only eat one thing," "eating less but still bloated, here is the fix." Research: highest-ROI 2026 angle; FDA sent 50+ warning letters, never name Ozempic/Wegovy. SHA: 31 GLP-1 winners; dedicated glp-1 LP serves 39; GLP1 statics keep at 48% (best of any named angle); SH-13107 GLP1 headline variant still live at 1.40 ROAS. Apply: GLP-1 as category term only; route the benefit through fiber deficit, cravings, constipation.

5. **Skew headline stacks to solution-aware and most-aware framings.** Research: match hook to Schwartz awareness stage. SHA (BQ60 formula slice on statics): Solution Aware 1.02 at $86k and Most Aware 0.98 at $115k carry the account; Problem Aware runs 0.56 at $83k and is where the dead weight lives. Apply: a 10-headline stack should be mostly solution/most-aware; ration problem-aware openers.

6. **Body-function disruption beat first, stat stack third.** "Can't poop? Eat this candy." Research: 9pm cravings and regularity as conditioned loops. SHA: every top-5 TikTok GMV video uses a poop/body beat (top one $23.6k GMV); SH-13107 Poop Retro ran 1.18; poop-body statics keep at 47%, above the 33% static average. Apply: on Meta paid, wrap the disruption beat in an offer or scarcity frame or it wins attention and loses money.

7. **Founder confession or ratio-shock VSL, open on the confession, not the intro.** Never "Hey, I'm the founder of." Lead with a true admission or the ratio in second one. Research: "say one true thing" is the single highest-leverage founder technique; skip the intro (loses ~30% of impressions). SHA: Genetics VSL 1.31 ROAS at $116k and Dad Bod 1.19 are the two best scripts on the leaderboard; confession hook is top tier. Apply: route mechanism through fiber; the founder variant pipeline is thin (9 variants, 4 shipped), so more founder hook retros are cheap headroom.

8. **For whitelisting, cast the mom-creator archetype and weight performance over polish.** Kitchen, family occasions, unpolished, hedged CTA. Research: creator collective of 8 to 15 micro-creators on retainer, brief packages not scripts. SHA: whitelisting variants ship at 83%, the highest keep rate of ANY lane in the archive; Steph Joplin car-line ad won while breaking every UGC heuristic. Apply: tighten-the-hook edits are challenger tests, not fixes; the constraint here is creator supply, not test budget.

9. **Frame the switch as a breakup, not restriction.** "Been in a toxic relationship with your sweet tooth?" Permission framing ("you can have the candy AND the fiber") over prohibition ("no sugar, guilt-free"). Research: Boundary Model, restriction reactivates the craving loop; "guilt-free" is saturated wallpaper. SHA: the toxic-relationship primary text is the single most reused winner copy, on 51 winners plus 17 new-year variants. Apply: keep the breakup frame, iterate the second line.

10. **Budget variant volume by keep rate, because the machine burns variants by design.** Research: kill at 1.5x target CPA after $50 to 100; iterate on >$5k spend at target. SHA (variant mine): only 42% of tested variants ship (268 vs 367 abandoned); keep rate by format = video 65%, product image 33%, advertorial 11%; by test type = HookTest 71%, ImageTest 23%, CopyTest 22%. Apply: a static image test needs 3 to 4 variants per expected survivor, a hook retro barely more than 1; budget brief volume to that, not to a flat number.

11. **Hit the diversity bar; kill near-duplicates.** 40 to 50 net-new ads/month, genuinely distinct concepts (Persona, Desire, Awareness, Format, Environment), not 10 reskins of one. Research: Andromeda reads the creative to target, near-duplicates get zero spend; creative is targeting now. SHA: the 42% ship rate and falling variant efficiency (45% in 2025 to 38% in 2026) confirm the account is designed to burn variants; distinctness is the lever. Apply: if two live ads share 4 of 5 P.D.A.F.E. dimensions, cut one.

## THEORY-ONLY (strong research signal, not yet isolated in the SHA winner archive)

12. **Comment-reply hook ads.** Plant or pull a real skeptical comment, answer it on camera as the cold open. Research: the single strongest cross-cutting signal, flagged in Meta, TikTok, snacks and founder deep dives as the highest-trust near-zero-scripting format that feeds Andromeda's comment-density flywheel. SHA archive: no comment-reply pattern surfaces in the 14 winner shapes. Apply: run a dedicated comment-reply sprint off the 10 most-engaged comments as a labeled exploration bet; it is cheap to test and heavily backed by operator data, just unproven in this account.

Runner-up theory bets worth a labeled test, weaker evidence: identity-badge framing ("I'm a fiber person now," fibermaxxing) proven for BelliWelli ("Hot Girls Have IBS" 40% sales bump) but only the breakup frame is proven for SHA; sensory-first silent hook (crinkle, pull, sour-sugar close-up, then "26g of fiber") from crossmodal research; 9pm dayparted cue-congruent creative. All food-psychology-sound, none isolated as a SHA winner yet.

## Winner-data overlay the research corpus under-weights

The archive wins on four lanes the 2026-04 research barely covers. Do not drop these from briefs just because the research is quiet on them:

- **Offer + urgency wrapper** is the biggest dollar lane (80 winners, $114k); scarcity framing is trending up (1.10, 1.21 recent 30d). Wrap every angle in a concrete offer (46% off + 4 free gifts, $2.69/bag) plus a deadline.
- **Reason-why sale narrative** (apology letter, warehouse clearing, tariffs, goodbye) still works on video (57% keep) though the static version is nearly exhausted (14% keep).
- **Social proof via review count and retail sellout** ("almost four thousand Amazon reviews," "sold out at Target") works spoken in video (75% keep) but the review-verbatim static lane is dead (0 of 13 shipped).
- **Seasonal retro** (NYNM, Halloween, BFCM): calendar the reskins 2 sprints ahead, swap wrapper and imagery, keep the proven body.

## Ops footer (from research 01/04, consistent with the archive)

Grade creative on hook rate AND hold rate AND contribution-margin ROAS, never hook rate alone (target >35% hook, >30% hold). Platform ROAS lost trust; the SHA blended 60d is ~0.77 and CM-ROAS is the real axis. The stat stack (26g fiber / 70 cal / 3g sugar / 3g net carbs) is non-negotiable and verbatim in every script.

## Caveats

- BQ per-angle ROAS is statics-only; all 60d video spend is angle-untagged, so video angle claims rest on the archive/TikTok layers.
- "Winner" and "shipped variant" mean graduated/survived-the-test, not profitable; these rank relative winners.
- The research corpus is dated 2026-04-30 and the winner cross-check is 2026-07-04. Re-distill after H2; offer-lane share of winners is already falling (55/201 in 2025 vs 20/129 in 2026) while fiber holds.
