Distilled 2026-07-02 from `winners.jsonl` (334 `mb - winner` parent tasks, 2025 S12 to 2026 S24), spend snapshots (parsed for 251/334), and the wiki performance layer. Evidence counts are from the archive unless labeled BQ (BigQuery last-60d ROAS), TT (TikTok Shop GMV catalog), or wiki. Read with the caveats at the bottom.

**Why:** winner briefs keep getting written from scratch or from theory. The archive shows the same 14 shapes winning over and over. Briefing against these shapes first is the highest-probability path to the next winner.

**How to apply (global):** when writing any Shameless brief, hook batch, or critique, check the draft against this list. If it matches zero patterns, that is a deliberate exploration bet and should be labeled as one, not the default.

## 1. Iterate winners, don't hunt fresh concepts
Evidence: 144/334 winners (43%) carry a Retro token; 19 of the top 50 by spend are retros; 12 more carry Proven.
Apply: default brief = one variable changed on a live winner (image, headline, hook, intro). New concepts are the minority lane, sized accordingly.

## 2. Static image + headline-stack test is the winner factory
Evidence: 145/334 winners are static or image tests vs 56 video-ish. Test types: ImageTest 89, CopyTest 24, HeadlineTest 16, HookTest 7. BQ: statics hold most top-ROAS slots. Weekly report 2026-06-22: every winner but one was a static.
Apply: the standard winner machine is 10 headlines applied to a proven layout (Problem-Headline-Benefit). Ship these weekly before commissioning video.

## 3. Offer + urgency wrapper is the biggest dollar lane
Evidence: 80 winners, $114k tracked spend, the largest cluster. Top single winner: NewOffer4FREEGifts at $36k. BQ: Scarcity 1.01 ROAS at $118k, Direct Offer 1.04. Recurring brief headlines: "UP TO 46% OFF + FREE GIFTS", "JUST $2.69 PER BAG", "SALE IS ENDING TONIGHT".
Apply: wrap every angle in a concrete offer (46% off + 4 free gifts, $2.69/bag) plus a deadline. Bare problem or curiosity ads without the wrapper run 0.50 to 0.62 ROAS (BQ).

## 4. Reason-why sale narrative beats naked discounts
Evidence: WeAreSorry letter 14 winners, Warehouse/Packing 5, tariff price-cut 3. "WE'RE SAYING GOODBYE" appears in 13 briefs, "LAST 2 PALLETS SELLING OUT" in 7.
Apply: give the discount a story (apology letter, warehouse clearing, tariffs, goodbye). The excuse makes the scarcity believable; refresh the excuse, keep the mechanic.

## 5. Fiber-shock comparison headline
Evidence: 75 fiber-angle winners, $58k tracked. "MORE FIBER THAN 3 CUPS OF BROCCOLI" recurs 8x in briefs; FiberThere'sNoExcuse is the #2 all-time spender ($26k). "Same bag, different math" opener on 10 winners.
Apply: lead statics with fiber quantified against a known food (broccoli cups, bowls of oatmeal). The number vs candy is the hook; keep 26g canonical.

## 6. Body-function disruption hook, offer-wrapped on paid
Evidence: every top-5 GMV TikTok video uses a poop/body beat (TT: "Can't poop? Eat this candy" $23.6k GMV). "WHEN POOP FEELS LIKE A BRICK" recurs in briefs; SH-13107 Poop Retro ran 1.18 ROAS (wiki weekly). BQ caveat: bare Problem-Solution is 0.62.
Apply: disruption beat goes first, stat stack third. On Meta paid, pair it with the offer wrapper (pattern 3) or it wins attention and loses money.

## 7. Toxic-relationship breakup copy frame
Evidence: the single most reused winner primary text: "Been in a toxic relationship with your sweet tooth?" on 51 winners, plus 17 more on the new-year variant. Goodbye Letter script ("I ghosted Metamucil") extends it to the supplement enemy.
Apply: frame the switch as a breakup (with sugar, with fiber powder, with empty calories). Proven primary text; iterate the second line, keep the frame.

## 8. QVC / live-shopping excerpt format
Evidence: 18 winners, $42k tracked; the single QVC testimonial video is #3 all-time ($21k); QVC hook-test retro added $8k. Leaderboard: 0.88 ROAS at $69k spend.
Apply: repurpose live-shopping-style demo + host enthusiasm clips as paid video. Retro the intro and hooks, not the body.

## 9. Founder personal-stakes VSL
Evidence: Genetics 1.31 ROAS at $116k and Dad Bod 1.19 at $38k are the two best scripts on the leaderboard (wiki). Motion benchmark: confession hook is top tier on hit rate and spend use.
Apply: founder-voice with personal stakes (genetics, dad bod) outranks brand story. Open on confession, route mechanism through fiber.

## 10. The 7-beat winning structure is load-bearing
Evidence: constant across top scripts (wiki leaderboard): hook, tension, in-narrative product reveal, stat stack (26g fiber / 70 cal / 3g sugar / 3g net carbs), mechanism, social proof, CTA. Missing reveal, stats, or CTA correlates with lower ROAS; TT catalog shows the same 5-beat arc on every video over $3k GMV.
Apply: audit every script against the beats before shipping; the stat stack is non-negotiable and verbatim.

## 11. GLP-1 consequence lane, compliance-safe
Evidence: 31 GLP1/weight-loss winners; the dedicated glp-1 LP serves 39 winners; "26g of Fiber for GLP-1 Support" headline on 5.
Apply: address the audience via the consequence (fiber deficit, cravings, constipation) not the drug. GLP-1 as category term only, never drug names, per compliance-guardrails.

## 12. Mom-creator rambling UGC archetype (whitelisting)
Evidence: 50 UGC/TikTok-lane winners incl. 18 WL tokens; monthly Tiktok_UGC batches keep graduating. Steph Joplin car-line ad (2:24, slow hook, hedged CTA) won while breaking every UGC heuristic.
Apply: for WL assets, cast the archetype (mom, kitchen, family occasions, unpolished) and weight performance over structure. Tighten-the-hook edits are challenger tests, not fixes.

## 13. Social proof via Amazon reviews and retail sellout
Evidence: "Almost four thousand glowing Amazon reviews" opener on 12 winners; "sold out at Target for the 3rd time" on 10; IABSOLUTELLYLOVE review-verbatim statics: 8 winners.
Apply: cite countable proof (review count, Amazon rank, Target sellouts) as the trust beat. Review-verbatim statics are a cheap standing test lane.

## 14. Seasonal moment retro
Evidence: 26 seasonal winners (NewYearNewMe 9, Christmas, Carnival, fall sale opener on 48). These are mostly retros of proven creative reskinned to the moment.
Apply: calendar the reskins (NYNM, Halloween, BFCM, Valentines) 2 sprints ahead; swap wrapper and imagery, keep the proven body.

## Caveats
- "Winner" = graduated ClickUp status (spend threshold), not profitability. Account was below break-even in Jun 2026 (cmROAS 0.57), so these rank relative winners.
- Spend parsed for 251/334 records; 83 have no snapshot, so evidence counts are firmer than dollar totals.
- BQ angle ROAS is a generic classifier over last 60d, a different axis from these hand-derived patterns.
- 2026 mix is shifting: offer-lane share of winners fell (55/201 in 2025 vs 20/129 in 2026) while fiber held; re-run this distill after H2.
