## Subscription

- Every 4 weeks auto-refill
- Flavor-picker model ("Select Flavors") — not a pre-built variety pack
- Adjust flavors, shipments, or cancel anytime
- "Up to 46% OFF First Order" (factors in free gifts value, not purely per-bag discount)

## Ad Copy Guardrails (Pricing-Specific)

**Approved:**
- "Subscribe and save $1.30 per bag"
- "Free shipping on all orders"
- "Up to 46% off your first order"
- "Just $2.69 per bag on subscribe & save"

**Avoid:**
- "50% off" or any discount > 46%
- "Free [gift]" without "with first subscription order"
- Implying free shipping has a minimum
- Suggesting 33% discount applies to one-time purchases

## Offer Templates for Campaigns

| Offer | When to Use | Copy Template |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| First-order subscription | Cold traffic, trial | "Subscribe & save 33% — up to 46% off with gifts on your first order" |
| Subscription value stack | Warm traffic, retention | "$2.69/bag, free shipping, cancel anytime" |
| One-time purchase | Quick trial, promo-sensitive | "$3.50 per bag + free shipping — no commitment" |
