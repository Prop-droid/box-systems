# Fiber-First Positioning

**Summary**: Primary marketing angle directive for Shameless Snacks -- lead with "daily dose of fiber" and the 26g hero stat; all sub-angles (weight loss, GLP-1, keto, diabetic) route through fiber as the mechanism.

**Sources**: Shameless_Snacks_brand_context.md, compliance_guide.md, amazon_reviews.md, Shameless_Q4_2025_Creative_Strategy_Review.md, SHA Q2-20260416153520.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-16

---

**Primary marketing angle directive set 2026-04-15.** Shameless Snacks is positioned as the user's **daily fiber intake / daily dose of fiber** — 26g of prebiotic fiber per bag, in candy form. Every creative lead defaults to this frame unless a specific segment demands otherwise.

## The Hero Stat

**26g of prebiotic fiber per bag** = ~90% of daily value in one bag.

For reference:
- Most Americans get < 15g of fiber per day
- [[competitive-landscape|SmartSweets]] delivers ~13g per bag
- [[competitive-landscape|Metamucil gummies]] deliver ~5g per serving
- **Shameless delivers 26g in a bag that tastes like candy**

## The Fibermaxxing Moment (2025-2026)

Fibermaxxing is the macro trend that makes Shameless a category-defining brand, not just a better candy. In late 2025 PepsiCo's CEO publicly called fiber "the next protein," which puts Shameless in line with the mainstream macro-nutrient shift of the decade. (source: [CNBC, Dec 2025](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/12/fibermaxxing-high-fiber-foods-pepsi-nestle-olipop.html))

Supporting signals:
- Whole Foods listed "more fiber-forward callouts on packaging" as a top 2026 trend. (source: [Whole Foods 2026 Trend Predictions](https://www.wholefoodsmarket.com/trends/the-next-big-things-our-top-8-food-trend-predictions-for-2026))
- Thrive Market saw fiber search terms surge 30% year over year.
- 52% of consumers said they would try fibermaxxing after learning about it. (source: [CNN Business, Jan 2026](https://www.cnn.com/2026/01/25/food/fiber-food-trends-2026))

The conversations are about broccoli, legumes, and oats. Shameless is fibermaxxing for people who hate broccoli. That is the single creative positioning sentence this trend unlocks.

### Fiber gap facts (sourced, as of Q4 2025)
- 95% of Americans do not hit daily fiber targets. (source: [Health Crunch, Oct 2025](https://healthcrunch.org/articles/2025-10-09-fiber-gap))
- Only 5% hit daily fiber targets consistently.
- 24% of Americans believe meat contains fiber, a core knowledge gap rather than a desire gap. (source: [IFIC Survey, Oct 2025](https://ific.org/research/perceptions-fiber-whole-grains/))
- 37% do not know how much fiber they consume daily. (source: [Prepared Foods / IFIC](https://www.preparedfoods.com/articles/131360-ific-survey-finds-fiber-knowledge-gap))

This is a knowledge gap, not a desire gap. The education angle is wide open.

## The Category Reframe

Not *candy that happens to have fiber*.
**Fiber that happens to taste like candy.**

This reframe repositions the product out of the low-sugar-candy fight (SmartSweets, Behave, Joyride) and into the fiber-supplement category (Metamucil, Benefiber) where Shameless wins decisively on format and dose.

These stats **support** the fiber lead — they don't replace it.

## Cultural Proof: The Trader Joe's Gummy Worm Moment

TJ's high-fiber gummy worms went viral in 2025. Reddit users reported they "cleaned out fast," and customers started coming in specifically asking for them because of the gut effect. One employee said: "I warned a customer about the high fiber content and she said, 'that's why I'm buying them.'" (source: [VegNews](https://vegnews.com/trader-joes-gummy-worms-side-effects), [Sporked](https://sporked.com/article/trader-joes-gummy-worms/))

Why this matters for Shameless: consumers will self-select a candy specifically for its gut effect. The social permission to talk about fiber and gut function is already there. Shameless is the version designed to taste good.

## Sub-Angle Routing — Everything Goes Through Fiber

All sub-angles route through the fiber mechanism. Fiber is the *why* behind the outcome claim.

| Sub-Angle | Fiber as Mechanism |
|---|---|
| Weight loss / goals | Fiber → satiety → fewer cravings → natural outcome |
| GLP-1 / digestion | Fiber → regularity → relief for slowed digestion |
| Keto / low-carb | Fiber → fills the fiber gap keto creates, solves keto constipation |
| Diabetic / blood-sugar-conscious | Fiber + low sugar → low glycemic impact ("only 3g sugar") |
| Sweet-tooth / indulgence | Fiber → permission to eat the whole bag without the guilt spiral |

Never lead with the outcome without naming fiber as the mechanism. "Kills cravings" becomes "26g of fiber kills cravings." "Keeps you full" becomes "26g of fiber keeps you full."

## Q1 2026 Performance Validation

The Q2 Creative Strategy Plan (90-day analysis, Dec 2025 – Mar 2026) adds a format-level caution that qualifies the Q4 enthusiasm:

| Metric | High Fiber / Health Hack Theme | Account Benchmark |
| ------ | ------------------------------ | ----------------- |
| Spend | ~$145K | — |
| ROAS | 1.02x | 1.07x (90-day flat) |
| CPA | $64.80 | — |
| CVR | 2.3% | — |
| Hold rate | 6.69% | 12–15% target |

**The message is proven. The format is exhausted.**

The "26g fiber" stat hook has been tested in **14 variants of the same UGC format** with the same creator demographic. The format ceiling has been hit — ROAS has been flat at 1.07x for 90 days despite continued spend, and hold rate of 6.69% is well below the 12–15% benchmark.

**What this means in practice:**

- Fiber-first as a **message** is validated — customers respond to the 26g stat, the daily-dose framing, and the health-hack positioning
- Fiber-first as a **video format** needs fresh execution — new creators (40–55 female), new visual hooks, stronger body sections
- The fiber stat works best as a **secondary proof point layered into other angles** ([[creative-angles]] — GLP-1, satiety, guilt-free) rather than as the standalone video hook
- The hook rate data in [[hook-framework]] (Stat Shock pattern) confirms: stat hooks pull attention but don't always hold it through the body

See [[shameless-creative-testing-framework]] for how to diagnose a format-exhausted angle vs. a message-exhausted angle. See [[icp-creative-matrix]] for the audience placement data (45–65 female on Facebook) that should inform new creator briefs.

## When NOT to Lead with Fiber

Fiber-first is the default, not a mandate. Exceptions:

- **Retargeting (warm):** Audience already knows the product; lead with offer, guarantee, or social proof
- **Urgency / scarcity statics** ([[shameless-creative-strategy]] Lane 1): sale messaging leads, fiber is reinforcement
- **Deep-skeptic taste cold traffic:** Lead with taste-proof, reinforce with fiber

## Compliance Intersection

Fiber-first unlocks many previously-restricted angles by giving them a legitimate mechanism:

- **Satiety** — permitted — fiber is the mechanism
- **Craving control** — permitted — fiber is the mechanism
- **Weight management narrative** — permitted — fiber supports via satiety
- **"Natural appetite suppressant"** — permitted when paired with fiber context

Still hard-stopped regardless of fiber framing:
- Drug names, "melt fat," disease claims, body-transformation imagery
